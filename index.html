I am a PhD student (2015- ) in Political Science at the University of Gothenburg affiliated with the Program on Governance and Local Development. I am currently visiting the Center on the Politics of Development (CPD) at UC Berkeley.

My research is broadly focused on Comparative Politics and Political Economy.

My research tries to understand how people make inferences in the context of politics. Especially, I am interested in how voters evaluate government performance and how this influences political accountability and electoral selection.

Currently, I study the political consequences of a Community Driven Development in the Philippines and a natural disaster in Malawi.
