I am a PhD student (2015- ) in Political Science at the University of Gothenburg affiliated with the Program on Governance and Local Development. I am currently visiting the Center on the Politics of Development (CPD) at UC Berkeley.

My research is broadly focused on Comparative Politics and Political Economy. I try to understand how people make inferences in the context of politics. Especially, I am interested in how voters evaluate government performance and how this influences political accountability and electoral selection.

Currently, I study the political consequences of a Community Driven Development Program in the Philippines and a natural disaster in Malawi.
