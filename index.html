I am a PhD student (2015- ) in Political Science at the University of Gothenburg affiliated with the Program on Governance and Local Development (www.gld.gu.se).

My research is broadly focused on Comparative Politics and on the Political Economy of Development.

My research tries to understand why political inequalities exist and persist. I am interested in how economic, social, and psychological factors shape people's political preferences and behavior.

Currently, I study the psychological constraints to civic engagement in developing countries.
