About
I am a PhD student (2015- ) in Political Science at the University of Gothenburg affiliated with the Program on Governance and Local Development (www.gld.gu.se).

My research is broadly focused on Comparative Politics and on the Political Economy of Development. I study the psychological constraints to civic engagement in developing countries.
